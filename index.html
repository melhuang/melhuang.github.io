blueprint

The loves of my life. Blueprint envisions a world where the good, passionate, and visionary have the biggest impact on our communities and society. Blueprint is a non-profit organization aimed at bringing technologies to local nonprofit organizations that are making social change. We want to aid and be part of the group of community builders who are doing meaningful productive work. Each semester, teams of five students work with non-profit organizations to implement technologies for efficiency and accessibility. We work on web and mobile applications for our clients to help them address and solve some of their systemic problems. Check us out here.
